World country population in 2010

In this visualisation i have chosen for the color green to visualise the country population. When you hover over the data the edges of the countries change to black and the countryname and the population (if available) will show in a label. I have chosen to visualise it in a choropleth because it shows the difference between the values. because the map click functions didnt work i made a last minute change and linked the pie chart to a bar chart. The pie chart shows the total number of man and woman in a country. When you click on the pie chart on the man or woman half a bar chart with the total man or woman population will appear. Bronnen:
