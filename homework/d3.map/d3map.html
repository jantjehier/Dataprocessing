World country area's

In this visualisation i have chosen for the color green to visualise the country area's. When you hover over the data the edges of the countries change to black and the countryname and the area (if available) will show in a label. I have chosen to visualise it in a choropleth because it shows the difference between the values. The data shows that this map is a lie. For excample: Greenland has an area of 2166km2 and the size is bigger than any country in africa. But when you look at the area of only Algeria you see that Algaria is bigger than Greenland. Bron:
